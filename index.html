About the Summit

The India–AI Impact Summit 2026 is a global platform focused on responsible, inclusive, and human-centric artificial intelligence. As the first major AI summit in the Global South, it brings together policymakers, industry leaders, and innovators to drive measurable outcomes across key sectors such as agriculture, healthcare, manufacturing, mobility, and BFSI.
